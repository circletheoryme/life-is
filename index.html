The home of my Circle Theory of Life by : Harry McKinney

Ever wonder what the "big picture" for life is? I do too, this theory is backed by personal research and theories that I have collected over the years. My first year in college I was required to take a philosophy class, it started with the most basic of all questions..... "Is life a straight line or a circle?"; the assignmenet was to write an eight page paper explaining your "position" on the subject.

Seems like an easy exercise...right? If you are thinking that then I suggest you try it yourself. Prior to this assignment I had believed, (and believed firmly), that life was undoubtably a straight line, a series of events that occured due to happenstance, free will and opportunites based on external environment.

So I did what anyone would do, I started drafting this paper. Something happened along the way though... About five pages in , I realized that life wasn't a straight line afterall, I also relaized that it wasn't a circle either. Instead I had a "euruka moment", I realized that life can be proved as a series of cirlcles.

In each stage of our lives we are given obstacles to overcome, if we give up we are doomed to repeat that cycle and to "live in that circle" as a type of purgatory, but once we conquer that challenge we move to another adjacent circle, we get a brand new challenge and we grow as human beings.
